About Us

A safe space to talk, reflect & grow

Raha Counselling Centre was founded on a simple belief: everyone deserves a space where they feel truly heard. Our name, Raha, means peace and comfort in Arabic — and that is exactly what we strive to provide.

We offer professional, confidential counselling for students, women, and men. Whether you are navigating academic pressure, personal challenges, work stress, or simply seeking clarity — our compassionate counsellors are here to walk alongside you.

Non-judgmental, compassionate guidance

FAQ

Common questions answered

Everything you need to know before your first session.

Is everything I share kept confidential?
▾
Absolutely. All sessions are strictly confidential. Nothing you share will be disclosed to anyone without your explicit written consent, except where required by law for safety reasons.
How do I book my first appointment?
▾
Simply contact us by phone, email, or through our contact section. We will warmly assist you in finding a convenient time and session format that suits you.
How long is each counselling session?
▾
Standard sessions are 50–60 minutes. Extended sessions can be arranged upon request. The number of sessions recommended depends on your individual needs and goals.
Can I choose a male or female counsellor?
▾
Yes, absolutely. We have both male and female counsellors available, and you are welcome to choose whichever you feel most comfortable with. Your comfort is our priority.
Are online sessions as effective as in-person?
▾
Yes. Research shows that online counselling can be just as effective as face-to-face sessions. Many clients prefer online sessions for the added comfort and flexibility they provide.
Do I need a referral to book a session?
▾
No referral is needed. Anyone can reach out and book directly with us. There is no waiting list or formal assessment required to begin your counselling journey.
